The Gaelic Athletic Assosiation, better known as the GAA is a series of sports created by Irish people that are played throughout Ireland. The GAA was founded in 1884 and remains to be the most popular group of sports amongst the Irish. The GAA consists of three main sports.

  • Hurling and Camogie
  • Gaelic Football
  • Handball
